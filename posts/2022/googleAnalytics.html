    # vuepress 블로그 google analytics

    https://github.com/vuejs/vuepress/issues/2713 (opens new window)

    https://stackoverflow.com/questions/62563531/how-can-i-add-google-analytics-to-my-vuepress-website-in-accordance-with-gdpr (opens new window)
